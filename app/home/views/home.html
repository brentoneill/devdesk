devdesk features:

Dev Desk generates common project documents like estimates, contracts, and invoices and then email them to your clients straight from devdesk.

Got a client with an overflowing inbox? Send a reminder and be the straw that breaks the camel's back.

Instead of tracking time with a separate application, Dev Desk features a timer where you simply select your project, select the task, and click start.

Never worry about tracking deliverables via messy spreadsheets and or handwritten notes -- spend less time managing your project and more time working on it!
